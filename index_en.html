AI Mapping Brain Tumor

In this project, I used TensorFlow and Keras to create a CNN that segments brain tumors in magnetic resonance images. I manipulate the data and prepare it for training with the IoU loss function. I chose U-Net among ResNet, U-Net, and Mask R-CNN to demonstrate skills in creating a more complex neural network. At the end of training, the model achieves 89% accuracy on the Dice coefficient, demonstrating its effectiveness in precise brain tumor segmentation.
